About us

The inception of Theodore is a divine intervention. We found ourselves having a recurring debate about the rarity of variety in clothing for men. We set out to research and conceptualize how we ought to battle conformity with colors, flattering fits, and a wave of possibilities for crafting elevated classics that have an identity by design and function. With an erstwhile love for the quality of looms, we hit the sweet spot. We realized that there is divinity in diversity.
